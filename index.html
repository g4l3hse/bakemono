    WHAT IS AUGMENTED REALITY

    Augmented reality (AR) is a technology that allows virtual objects and digital information to be overlaid onto the real world.

    This is typically achieved through the use of a camera and a computer-generated image or video feed. AR can be experienced on a variety of devices, such as smartphones, tablets, and smart glasses. The technology has a range of applications, including entertainment, advertising, education, and training. AR offers a unique way to interact with the world around us, blurring the lines between the physical and digital realms.

    There are many apps and platforms where you can play with Augmented Reality (AR) such as Snapchat, Instagram, Google Lens, ARKit, ARCore and etc.
